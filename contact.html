Frequently asked questions

What download speed do I require?

Minimum of 2 Mbps download

How can a I check my download speed?
  1. Connect to your computer to your router using an Ethernet cable.
  2. Open your web browser.
  3. Navigate to www.speedtest.net.
  4. Tap “Go.”
Is online course effective?

Yes, you just need to have a stable internet connection. It will save a lot of time of yours to commute and you can record the class as well for future reference.

Do I need to download any online meeting platform like Zoom, Skype or Google meet?

No, you can just click on the shared link and join the class directly.

Do you offer the classes across the globe?

Yes! I have been teaching online since last 15 years and I have successfully coached the students from: Australia, Bahrain, Canada, China, Egypt, France, Germany , Hong Kong, India, Japan, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Malaysia, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Portugal, Qatar, Saudi Arabia , Singapore, Spain, Sri-Lanka, South Korea, United Arab Emirates , USA and UK.

How do I pay for online tuition?

You can pay by bank transfer or with the payment link through our website

How can I subscribe to the plans given on the website?

You can easily get the subscription by clicking on the plan if it’s free. If you want to enroll yourself in a paid subscription plan you will be guided to the payment link.

How can I become a part of Mindful Living community?

Our mission is to provide high quality education at an affordable price to our students. Hence, we offer both free and paid resources through our different subscription plans.

Can I log in from multiple devices?

We recommend you to log in from any single device for security purpose. However, you can log out from one device and can log in again from another device.

Which device is compatible for the online classes?

We are fully compatible and optimized with all the devices; desktops, laptops, tablets, mobile etc.

Does Mindful Living provide one on one or group classes?

We provide both the options to avail one-on-one and group classes depending on your suitability.
